### Introduction to AI:
* **[Artificial Intelligence: A Modern Approach (AIMA)](https://people.engr.tamu.edu/guni/csce642/files/AI_Russell_Norvig.pdf)** by Stuart Russell and Peter Norvig: The definitive textbook covering all topics from agents and search to logic and uncertainty.
  A book available publicly.
* **[Reinforcement Learning: An Introduction](https://web.stanford.edu/class/psych209/Readings/SuttonBartoIPRLBook2ndEd.pdf)** by Richard S. Sutton and Andrew G. Barto: Essential for understanding the theoretical foundation of Reinforcement Learning, including Markov Decision Processes (MDPs) and related concepts.
