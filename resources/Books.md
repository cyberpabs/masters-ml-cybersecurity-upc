### Introduction to AI:
* **Artificial Intelligence: A Modern Approach (AIMA)** by Stuart Russell and Peter Norvig: The definitive textbook covering all topics from agents and search to logic and uncertainty.
* **Reinforcement Learning: An Introduction** by Richard S. Sutton and Andrew G. Barto: Essential for understanding the theoretical foundation of Reinforcement Learning, including Markov Decision Processes (MDPs) and related concepts.
